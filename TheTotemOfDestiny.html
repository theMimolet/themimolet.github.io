About this game

In the near future, you are a secret agent and your mission is as follows: you must infiltrate the secret W.I.C.K.E.D. base. in the Amazon to recover the artifact which allowed their leader to control the capybaras and could give a person a mysterious power.

Will you succeed in your mission?

This game was created as part of a group project for Ludus Académie.

How to play

  • Use the directional arrows or ZSQD (or WASD) to move
  • Orient the agent by moving the cursor
  • Press left click to interact/type
  • Press right click or CTRL to crouch

Infiltrate the secret base and find the Golden Totem!
